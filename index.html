Privacy Policy for NextScholar

Effective Date: May 14, 2025

1. Data Collection

NextScholar does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to the developer or any third party. All information you enter into the extension (such as scholarship names, deadlines, notes, and progress) is stored locally on your device using your browser's local storage.

2. Data Usage

All data entered into NextScholar is used solely for the purpose of helping you track and manage your scholarship applications. The extension does not access, use, or share your data for any other purpose.

3. Data Sharing

Your data is never shared with the developer or any third party. The only exception is if you choose to use the "Sync to Google Calendar" feature, in which case only the relevant scholarship deadline information you select is sent directly to Google Calendar via Google's secure API. No data is sent to the developer.

4. Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and is not accessible to the developer. If you uninstall the extension or clear your browser's local storage, your data will be permanently deleted.

5. Analytics and Tracking

NextScholar does not use any analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, you will be notified via the extension's update notes and the updated policy will be posted here.

7. Contact
